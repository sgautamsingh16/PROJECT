Company

Welcome to ProEntertainment, the largest and the most premium film exhibitor in India with 1756 screens across 113 cities (India and Sri Lanka) with 362 properties and an aggregate seating capacity of 3.62 lakh seats. Over the years, we have consistently increased our screen count, both organically and inorganically, through strategic investments and acquisitions which includes ‘Cinemax Cinemas’ in November 2012, ‘DT Cinemas’ in May 2016, ‘SPI Cinemas’ in August 2018. We recently completed the merger with INOX Leisure Limited, which has added to our storied history of becoming game changers in the film exhibition industry for over 25 years and transforming the out of home entertainment in the country.

Our mission is to advance and reimagine the movie-going experience by continuing to reinvent ourselves in keeping up with the times and the ever-changing entertainment landscape to make our brand aspirational and accessible. We take pride in our strategically located cinemas and we continuously invest in introducing premium formats, comfortable seating, sound, projection, ambience, and food & beverage to meet evolving consumer expectations so that our patrons have a memorable experience every time they visit our cinemas.

We serve as a conduit between consumers and the film industry on one hand and the retail industry and real estate development on the other. As a leading player in the film exhibition industry, our company is spearheading the establishment of a robust ecosystem that brings together key partners, including filmmakers, studios, content providers, equipment and concession manufacturers, data and technology companies, all of whom rely on the strength of our business and the communities we serve. We engage with multiple channels to connect with our over 180 million patrons through research, loyalty, online, digital, and offline modes to provide a platform to showcase film and non-film content and identifying new trends in the film exhibition industry.

Mission

We believe that the cinema is more than just a place to watch movies; it's a portal to different worlds, a catalyst for emotions, and a shared experience that brings people together. Our mission is to create an environment where every visit is a memorable journey. We strive to offer a diverse and inclusive selection of films that cater to a wide range of audiences, from the latest blockbusters to independent art-house cinema. By curating a rich and varied programming, we aim to inspire, entertain, and educate our patrons. Beyond the screen, we are committed to providing exceptional customer service. Our dedicated team is passionate about creating a welcoming and comfortable atmosphere where guests can relax, socialize, and indulge in a variety of culinary delights. We believe that a superior cinematic experience encompasses not only the film itself but also the overall environment. We are dedicated to technological innovation, ensuring that our patrons have access to the latest advancements in sound and visual technology. By investing in state-of-the-art equipment and infrastructure, we deliver immersive and crystal-clear audio-visual experiences that transport our guests into the heart of the story. As a responsible corporate citizen, we are committed to sustainability and environmental stewardship. We strive to minimize our ecological footprint by implementing eco-friendly practices and supporting initiatives that promote a greener future. Ultimately, our mission is to enrich the lives of our community by providing a vibrant and dynamic entertainment destination. We are dedicated to fostering a love of film and creating lasting memories for generations to come. By fulfilling our mission, we aspire to be the preferred choice for moviegoers, a valued partner to the film industry, and a positive force within our community.

Vision

Our vision is to redefine the cinematic experience, transforming our multiplex into a vibrant cultural hub where communities come together to share laughter, tears, and inspiration. We aspire to be more than just a place to watch movies; we aim to create an environment that stimulates the senses, ignites the imagination, and leaves a lasting impression on every guest. By curating a diverse and inclusive selection of films, coupled with state-of-the-art technology and luxurious amenities, we will provide an unparalleled entertainment experience. Our commitment to excellence extends beyond the screen, encompassing exceptional customer service, a clean and inviting atmosphere, and a strong focus on community engagement. We envision a future where our multiplex is not only a source of entertainment but also a catalyst for social interaction, fostering a sense of belonging and shared experiences. Through strategic partnerships and innovative programming, we will create opportunities for local artists, filmmakers, and community organizations to showcase their talents and connect with our audience. Ultimately, our vision is to become a beloved institution in our community, a place where memories are made and cherished for years to come. We believe that by combining world-class entertainment with a genuine passion for our guests, we can create a truly exceptional cinematic experience that exceeds expectations and sets new industry standards.
